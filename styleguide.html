your guide to roller skating in the Hudson Valley

Learn more about the Hudson Valley Rail Trail.

Trail Info

  • Length: 11 miles
  • Out and back
  • Incline: not a lot of hills, but one small one near Route 9W in Highland.
  • Surface condition: Varies, nicer near Lloyd, NY - Tony Williams Park in Highland, and nearer to the Walkway Over the Hudson. Skates are allowed on the walkway and you can proceed to the 13 mile Dutchess Rail Trail.
